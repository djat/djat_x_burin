# Collaboration spine - Burin × Pathways

> *Accountability without agreement meets orchestration without a broker.*

## The invitation

This bundle is an **open invitation** to Cameron Sajedi and the Burin project to co-develop the **Presence Pathway** design space: Pathways licenses field-workflow *recipes*; Burin binds *where and when*; neither requires the other's commercial license for noncommercial use.

**Reciprocation:** optional. The bundle can reach `SEALED` on unilateral hypotheses alone.

## Why these two projects together

| Plane | Substrate | Question answered |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| Orchestration | Pathways + Aqua | What workflow ran, under what policy, with what lineage? |
| Presence | Burin | What commitment, at which equal-area cells, at which time? |
| Provenance root | Trust Key | How does a relying party verify and open back into full context offline? |

Neither orchestration nor presence alone delivers **licensable multi-party workflows with cryptographic binding, sibling PathwayRuns, and offline verify**. Together they open a design space that did not exist before.
